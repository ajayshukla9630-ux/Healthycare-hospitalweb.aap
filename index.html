FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

We Have Answers

What are your visiting hours?

Our visiting hours are from 10 AM to 10 PM daily. Special ICU visiting times are 10–11 AM and 5–6 PM. Emergency visits are allowed 24/7.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance providers including BlueCross, Aetna, Cigna, and Medicare. Contact our billing desk for full details on coverage.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book online through our website, call us at +123 456 7890, or visit our reception. Telemedicine appointments are also available anytime.

Is emergency care available 24/7?

Absolutely. Our emergency department and ambulance services are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year including holidays.

Do you have a pharmacy on-site?

Yes, we have a 24/7 in-house pharmacy stocked with all prescription and OTC medications. Home delivery is also available for regular patients.

Can I get telemedicine consultations?

Yes! We offer video consultations with our specialists around the clock. Book through our patient portal or call our helpline to schedule a session.
